## vMOONEY Roles
- **@Member:** Holder of any amount of vMOONEY. Can vote on MoonDAO proposals.
- **@Delegate:** Holder of at least 250,000 vMOONEY, and can officially submit proposals to the Senate on behalf of anyone.
