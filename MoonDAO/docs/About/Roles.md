## Stakeholder Roles
- **[[@Member]]:** Holder of any amount of staked MOONEY. Can vote on MoonDAO proposals.
- **[[@Delegate]]:** Staker of at least 250,000 MOONEY, and can officially submit proposals to the [Senate](Senate.md) on behalf of anyone.
