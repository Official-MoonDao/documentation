## Holder Roles
- **[[@Voter]]:** Holder of any amount of locked MOONEY. Can vote on MoonDAO proposals.
- **[[@Delegate]]:** Holder of at least 250,000 $vMOONEY, and can officially submit proposals to the [Senate](Senate.md) on behalf of anyone.
