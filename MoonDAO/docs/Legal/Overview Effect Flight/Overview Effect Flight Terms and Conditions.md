## 5. Merit-Based Selection

The Selection Process for the community seat is not a lottery, sweepstakes, raffle, or game of chance. It is a merit-based, multi-stage evaluation process primarily governed by the $OVERVIEW token, although certain stages incorporate additional evaluation criteria from independent sources including artificial intelligence evaluation, professional astronaut review, and $vMOONEY community governance voting.

At no stage of the Selection Process is advancement determined by random chance, luck, or automated random selection. Each round involves deliberate evaluation based on defined criteria, community support, demonstrated merit, and substantive review by qualified evaluators.

Receiving $OVERVIEW tokens in exchange for a contribution does not constitute purchasing a chance to win. The tokens confer governance rights. Candidacy is a separate process available only to eligible MoonDAO Citizens, and advancement through the Selection Process depends entirely on voting and merit-based performance across multiple evaluation stages.

## 7. The Selection Process

The Selection Process consists of four rounds designed to progressively narrow the field from all eligible MoonDAO Citizens to one finalist through a combination of community support, substantive evaluation, expert review, and governance voting.

### 7.1 Round 1: Community Fundraising

- **Objective:** Determine which Citizens have the broadest community support and ability to mobilize financial backing for the mission.  
- **Mechanism:** Contributors allocate (pledge or back) their $OVERVIEW tokens to one Candidate of their choosing. Votes are recorded on-chain and transparently verifiable. Contributors may change their vote up until the closing date. Voting does not use or burn $OVERVIEW tokens, which remain securely in the Contributor wallet.  
- **Visibility:** A public, real-time [leaderboard](https://moondao.com/overview-vote) displays rankings throughout the voting period.  
- **Advancement:** The 25 Citizens with the greatest amount of $OVERVIEW tokens pledged to their name advance to Round 2.  
- **Timeline:** Voting closes no later than 14 days after the fundraiser end date.

### 7.2 Round 2: AI Evaluation

- **Objective:** Evaluate Candidates' openness, preparation, and potential impact through a written assessment.  
- **Prerequisite:** Before this round commences, all 25 advancing Candidates must complete KYC verification and provide a copy of their passport and/or proof of eligibility to travel to the United States. If any of the Candidates fail to complete the KYC or otherwise deemed ineligible, backup Candidates will be selected in order of runners-up voting until the 25 available spaces are filled.  
- **Task:** Each Candidate submits a written essay of no more than 1,000 words answering a specific written prompt about the Overview Effect. An AI model evaluates each essay on a scale of 1–10 across three or more criteria inspired by Frank White's Overview Effect formula.  
- **Advancement:** The 10 Citizens with the highest composite scores across those criteria advance to Round 3.  
- **Timeline:** No later than 4 weeks after advancing from the previous round: up to 2 weeks for KYC/passport/visa verification and essay submission, up to 2 weeks for AI evaluation and results.

### 7.3 Round 3: Astronaut Committee Review

- **Objective:** Expert evaluation by a group of astronauts to assess readiness, merit, and personal story.  
- **Task:** Each Candidate records a video responding to one or more prompts related to the Overview Effect and their candidacy.  
- **Evaluation:** A committee of professional and commercial astronauts individually ranks each Candidate from 1 to 10 based on who most deserves the opportunity to fly to space with Frank White.  
- **Advancement:** The 5 Citizens with the highest total scores from the astronaut committee advance to Round 4.  
- **Timeline:** No later than 4 weeks after advancing from the previous round: up to 2 weeks for video submission, up to 2 weeks for evaluations.

### 7.4 Round 4: Community Governance Vote

- **Objective:** Give the broader MoonDAO community final say in who flies with Frank White.  
- **Format:** Finalists participate in one or more public conversations with MoonDAO, broadcast in video to the community.  
- **Mechanism:** The MoonDAO community votes using $vMOONEY governance tokens with staked quadratic voting as outlined in the MoonDAO Constitution. The Citizen who receives the greatest amount of $vMOONEY votes is selected as the winner.  
- **Timeline:** No more than 2 weeks: 1 week for presentations before the MoonDAO community, 1 week for voting.

### 7.5 Discretion and Modifications

MoonDAO reserves the right to make reasonable modifications to the Selection Process structure, timeline, or criteria if necessary to ensure fairness, comply with legal requirements, or address unforeseen circumstances. Any material changes will be communicated to all Participants in advance.

## 8. Refund Policy

### 8.1 Campaign Failure — Minimum Goal Not Met (Below 241 ETH)

If the Campaign does not reach the Minimum Goal of 241 ETH (25% of the Campaign Goal) by the end date, the Campaign will be declared a failure. A refund payhook will be enabled by MoonDAO on the Smart Contract, after which refunds will be automatically redeemable by Contributors. To claim a refund, Contributors burn (return) their $OVERVIEW tokens through the MoonDAO Launchpad platform and receive their proportional ETH back, minus any gas or transaction fees.

The refund window will remain open for 28 days from the date the refund payhook is enabled. Contributors who do not claim their refund within this window may forfeit their right to a refund. MoonDAO will communicate the exact refund window dates through all official channels.

### 8.2 Minimum Goal Met — Mission Proceeds (241 ETH or Above)

If the Campaign raises at least 241 ETH (the Minimum Goal), the Campaign concludes successfully and funds are disbursed per Section 4.2. Once funds are disbursed, full refunds are no longer possible due to protocol fees, fund allocation, and carrier commitments. The mission profile will be determined by the Milestone Goal reached, as described in Section 4.3, and $OVERVIEW token holders will vote on specific mission-profile decisions.

At this stage, Contributors accept that their contribution has been committed to mission execution and that no automatic refund mechanism exists. Any subsequent decisions regarding partial refunds, fund reallocation, or alternative mission profiles will be made through $OVERVIEW token holder governance.

### 8.3 Frank White's Inability to Fly

If Frank White is medically unable to complete the spaceflight, as determined by the mission's flight surgeon, Dr. N. Stuart Harris of Massachusetts General Hospital, or the flight carrier, a full refund will not be possible if funds have already been committed to carrier contracts or mission execution. In this event, Contributors will be offered the following options via $OVERVIEW token holder governance vote: (a) a partial refund in ETH minus any fees and committed expenditures; (b) selection of a worthy replacement ambassador to fly in Frank's place; and/or (c) reallocation of funds to support the flight of the Candidate winner or other community members.

### 8.4 Refund Mechanism

Refunds, when available, are processed as follows: MoonDAO will manually enable a refund payhook on the Smart Contract. Once enabled, Contributors may burn their $OVERVIEW tokens through the MoonDAO Launchpad platform to automatically receive their proportional ETH back. This process is self-service and does not require manual approval once the payhook is active. The refund claim window will be open for 28 days from activation.

### 8.5 Refund Limitations

Refunds are processed in ETH regardless of the original method used to acquire ETH to contribute. MoonDAO and the Organizing Entity are not responsible for any changes in the value of ETH in fiat currency between the time of contribution and the time of refund. Transaction fees (gas fees) associated with claiming a refund or contributing are the responsibility of the Contributor. No fiat currency refunds will be issued under any circumstances.
